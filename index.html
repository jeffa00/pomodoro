What's a Pomodoro?

Why it's a tomato, silly.

Yep, I have written Sim Tomato.

OK, that's not true.

Pomodoro is Italian for "tomato", but in this context it refers to kitchen timers made to look like tomatoes.

Back in the '80s (aka, the dark ages) Francesco Cirillo devised a time-management technique to help with focus and productivity that used a kitchen timer that happened to look like a tomato.

Basics:

  • Pick work item
  • Set timer for 25 minutes(default)
  • Take 5 minute break at end
  • Repeat 4 times
  • 4 th break is longer (typically 15-30 minutes)
